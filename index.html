The Nissan GT-R

The Nissan Skyline GT-R is a japanese sports car first produced in 1969, the Hakosuka GT-R. Production stopped in 1973, but then resumed in 1989 all the way untill 2002. In 2007, Nissan unveiled a new sports car, the Nissan GT-R R35. This car was not called a skyline.

Production Years

The most famous GT-R models include the R32 (1989 to 1994), R33 (1995 - 1998), R34 (1998 to 2002), and the newest model, the R35 (2007 - present)

Throughought the histroy of the GT-R, the car has had a range of different 6 cylinder engines, all with different power figures.

  • 2.0 L S20 I6
  • 2.6 L twin-turbocharged RB26DETT I6
  • 2.8 L twin-turbocharged RB28DETT I6 (Z-Tune)
  • 3.8 L twin-turbocharged VR38DETT V6
